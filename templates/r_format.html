Type

the data you upload is created by Matlab or scipy.io.savemat() in Python,normally as xxx.mat

Format

including testing_result,true_result,location,such as 

scipy.io.savemat("whole_data.mat",{"testing_result": testing_result, "true_result": true_result,"location":location})

Detail

testing_result: labels that calculated by algorithm,an array of [0..count of the data],and the labels should be numbers

true_result: labels of real ,an array of [0..count of the data](same length with testing result),and the label should be numbers

location: location of each pixel,an array of [0..count of your data,[x_position,y_position]
