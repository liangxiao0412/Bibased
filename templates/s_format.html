Type

the data you upload is created by Matlab or scipy.io.savemat() in Python,normally as xxx.mat

Format

including x,y, it calls for locationwhen finding statistical features,such as 

scipy.io.savemat("whole_data.mat",{"x": x, "y": y,"location":location})

Detail

x: features of your data,an array of [0..count of your data,0..count of feature]

y: labels of your data,an array of [0..count of your data],and the labels should be numbers

location: location of each pixel,an array of [0..count of your data,[x_position,y_position]
